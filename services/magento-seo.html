Why Magento SEO Differs

Large Catalogs Require Strategic Prioritization and Technical Expertise

Magento powers massive e-commerce operations with thousands of products, complex category structures, and substantial technical complexity. Generic SEO approaches don't work at enterprise scale. Jannatul focuses on strategic prioritization—identifying which products and categories will generate the most revenue from organic search—and developing scalable approaches that leverage Magento's technical capabilities.

The goal is sustainable, scalable growth across your catalog without overwhelming implementation efforts or creating duplicate content problems.
