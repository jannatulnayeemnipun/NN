Client Profile

A 3-person family-owned plumbing service in suburban Colorado operating 6 years with steady referral-based business. Owner was excellent plumber but zero marketing knowledge. Monthly customer acquisitions flat at 10-12 leads from word-of-mouth only.

The Challenge: Invisible in Local Search

When customers searched "plumber near me" or "emergency plumbing [city]", they didn't appear. Competitors got 20-30+ leads monthly from local search. This business captured only 10-12 from referrals.

  • No Google Business Profile (completely invisible on Google Maps)
  • Zero online reviews (competitors had 40-80)
  • Outdated website from 2015
  • Missing 70-80% of addressable market
  • Owner overwhelmed by marketing complexity

Strategy: Consulting Model for Owner Empowerment

Rather than manage everything for them, we chose consulting approach: guide owner through implementation, build ownership, create long-term sustainability. Three-phase plan executed over 7 months.

Implementation: Simple 3-Phase Approach

Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2): Google Business Profile Optimization

Claimed and verified GBP. Added complete info, 15 high-quality photos, service descriptions, attributes. Result: Appeared in local 3-pack within 2 weeks. 15-20 additional leads immediately.

Phase 2 (Weeks 3-6): Review Generation System

Created simple postcards with QR code to Google Reviews. After each job, technician hands out postcard + text/email follow-ups + $10 incentive. Website modernized with testimonials and service pages.

Result: 22 reviews accumulated in 6 weeks. 4.7/5 stars. Website conversions up 40%.

Phase 3 (Weeks 7-12+): Local Content & Engagement

Published 4 blog posts targeting local keywords. Owner replies to every Google review weekly. Quarterly seasonal content published. Ongoing optimization and adjustments.

Result: Ranked #1 for 12+ local keywords. Sustained 40-45 monthly leads.

Key Takeaways

  • Google Business Profile optimization is 80% of local SEO. Reviews + GBP setup beats everything else.
  • Systematic review generation compounds: more reviews = better rankings = more jobs = more reviews (flywheel effect)
  • Consulting model builds long-term ownership. When owners implement themselves, they maintain it forever.
  • Local service customers are high-intent. Someone searching emergency plumber at 9pm needs solution NOW. 30-40% conversion rates.
  • Small budget, simple strategy beats complex perfection. Consistency implemented wins over perfection abandoned.
  • ROI is infinite. $85K annual revenue from minimal investment.
