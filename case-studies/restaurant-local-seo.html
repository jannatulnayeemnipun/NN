Client Profile

Fine dining restaurant with 2 years of solid reputation but limited visibility in local search. Getting 45-55 reservations/month mostly from past customers and word-of-mouth. Couldn't get found in Google Maps or search results.

The Challenge: Restaurant Discovery Problem

When customers search "fine dining near me" or "restaurants [area]", competitors dominated local results. Restaurant lost 80% of potential customers to competitors they were better than (but invisible to search).

  • Invisible in Google Maps (no GBP profile)
  • Zero online reviews (competitors had 100+)
  • Missing from restaurant directories (Yelp, OpenTable, etc.)
  • No menu optimization for search
  • Bad website (outdated design, no reservations)

Strategy: Local Discovery + Review Authority

Phase 1: Google Business Profile Optimization (Weeks 1-3)

Claimed and optimized GBP with perfect info, 20 high-quality food/ambiance photos, menu, attributes. Result: Appeared in Maps within 2 weeks.

Phase 2: Review Generation System (Weeks 2-6)

After each dining experience: table cards with QR code to Google Reviews + follow-up text + incentive. Accumulated 85 reviews with 4.8/5 rating in 6 months.

Phase 3: Multi-Platform Presence (Weeks 3-8)

Added restaurant to Yelp, OpenTable, TripAdvisor, local directories. NAP consistency across all platforms. Results: Appeared in more search contexts, easier for customers to find.

Phase 4: Website & Menu Optimization

Redesigned website highlighting cuisine type, ambiance, signature dishes. Optimized menu for "best [cuisine type]" and "fine dining [area]" searches.

Results

Reservations increased from 45-55/month to 195-210/month (310%). Walk-in traffic increased 230% from local search visibility. Now fully booked most weekends. Revenue increased $180K+ annually.

Key Insights

  • Restaurant discovery starts with Google Maps: GBP + reviews is 80% of restaurant discovery.
  • Reviews are trust signals: 85 reviews at 4.8 stars converts 3x better than 5 reviews at 5 stars.
  • Multi-platform presence essential: Customers search across Google, Yelp, OpenTable. Presence on all platforms = found everywhere.
  • Restaurant customers are highest-intent: Searching for restaurant = ready to book now. Conversion rates 40-50%.
