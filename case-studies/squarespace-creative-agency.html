Client Profile

Creative agency (web design, branding) on Squarespace. Beautiful portfolio but zero organic leads. Getting 4-5 leads/month from referrals only. Wanted to attract clients through search instead of relying on network.

The Challenge: Hidden Portfolio

Portfolio looked great but wasn't discoverable. When potential clients searched "web designer [area]" or "branding agency [city]", competitors appeared - this agency didn't.

  • No keyword targeting in portfolio descriptions
  • Portfolio pages not SEO-optimized
  • No blog or thought leadership content
  • No case studies with detailed results
  • Missing local keyword strategy

Strategy: Portfolio + Content Authority

Phase 1: Portfolio Optimization (Weeks 1-4)

Optimized 15 portfolio case studies with keyword-rich titles, detailed descriptions, before/after images, client results. Each now targets 3-5 related keywords.

Phase 2: Service Page Strategy (Weeks 3-6)

Created 4 service pages (Web Design, Branding, Logo Design, UX Strategy) optimized for commercial keywords.

Phase 3: Blog & Thought Leadership (Months 2-6)

Published 12 blog articles on design trends, creative process, case study breakdowns. Built authority in industry.

Phase 4: Local + Authority Building (Months 5-10)

Earned 22 backlinks from design blogs, client websites, industry publications. Positioned founder as design expert.

Results

Leads increased from 4-5/month to 18-22/month (380%). Average client value $12K+. Annual revenue increased $240K from organic. Now ranks for 24 commercial keywords.

Key Insights

  • Portfolio is your content asset: Optimize portfolio pages like blog posts. Each project can target 3-5 keywords.
  • Case studies build authority: Detailed case studies with results earn links and build credibility better than short descriptions.
  • Squarespace works for creative services: Visual portfolio + optimized text = competitive SEO for creative agencies.
  • Thought leadership converts: Blog articles positioning founder as expert generate high-quality leads that close at premium rates.
